Hello, My name is Mitchell Henschel

As a Bachelor of Data Science my strengths align with numbers and computers, my specialty being the management of big data. My preferred general-purpose programming language is python due to its ease of use and powerful data analysis tools. My most prodigious project over the course of my education was designing and managing a project which migrated a campus radio station’s music catalog from MS Access to a MySQL database built into a cloud based Django web application.

In my most recent position at the National Food Group I assisted in administering and customizing the company’s Netsuite System. This included writing Javascript scripts to send email alerts or fill form fields where certain conditions existed, developing new saved searches to extract information from sales data, and assisting employees in setting up dashboards and KPIs. Additionally, I have ample experience in customer service and collaboration with my coworkers.

If you’re looking for a creative problem solver with a motivation to continue learning then look no further. Please contact me with any questions via phone or email. Thank you in advance for your time and I hope to hear from you soon.
